# Case Study: AI in Industry

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming multiple industries by automating tasks, enhancing decision-making, and improving customer experience. This case study highlights AI applications, companies, and future trends.

## Key Applications
1. **Healthcare**
   - Disease diagnosis using machine learning
   - Drug discovery and personalized treatment

2. **Finance**
   - Fraud detection using AI algorithms
   - Algorithmic trading

3. **E-commerce**
   - Personalized recommendations
   - Chatbots for customer support

4. **Manufacturing**
   - Predictive maintenance
   - Robotics for assembly lines

## Example Companies
- **Google**: AI-driven search and recommendation engines
- **Amazon**: Personalized shopping recommendations, Alexa
- **IBM**: Watson AI solutions for enterprises
- **Tesla**: AI in self-driving cars

## Future Trends
- Increased adoption of AI in small businesses
- AI ethics and explainable AI gaining importance
- Edge AI: Running AI locally on devices
- Integration with IoT and 5G technologies

> This case study shows understanding of industry trends and AI applications, which is highly relevant for tech placements.
